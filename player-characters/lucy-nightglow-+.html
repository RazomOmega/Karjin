Lucy Nightglow +

Backstory

Lucy Nightglow was born and raised in Essuria as an only child. Since she had no siblings to play with and wasn't making many friends in school, she found herself adopting every stray animal on the street to befriend, to her mother's dismay. Since Lucy is an only child, her mother was fiercely protective over her, and did not educate her on the world beyond Essuria's walls and the devastating Lack that was ever expanding outwards. However, when on a stormy summer's day lightning struck in the suburbs, Lucy could not believe her eyes. The at that time 15 year old girl took her favourite pet Snuggles, a one-eyed mean looking old cat, and went to investigate. She had never thought that those loud lighting strikes she had seen in the distance could ever hit her here in Essuria. As she ran across the cobbled streets, she found a fire breaking out a peasant's home. She asked a passerby what could have happened, but the man just glared at her and told her the Lack was finally coming for them, the Lack that surrounded the city. Snuggles began to hiss and she bent down to calm him. Just as she was about to ask the man what the Lack was,  he'd already disappeared in the crowd that was starting to form.

It was then that she decided to look beyond the walls. She began plotting on how she would manage to go out and see this for herself. She'd heard some of the cool kids at school bragging on how they went outside the wall through a crack hidden in the wall in a poor district in the city. Late that night, she snuck out of the little house while her mum and dad slept, and scurried her way through the streets, Snuggles on her heels. She crept through the crack and when she stood up,  she was horrified. What laid before her was a dead waste. Her heart sank and tears came streaming down her face. She realized at that point that she must do something about it. Problem was, she had no idea what.

Then one day, as she was playing with the pigeons in the alley, she saw a poster on a wall of a certain Mammon Avari, asking for adventurers to help cleanse the Lack. She knew immediately what to do.

Her mother, Abigail Nightglow, owns a flower shop and grows her own vegetables. She's also the best cook in the world (according to Lucy) and has passed on some of her secret recipes to her. Although a bit overbearing, she's a kind and loving mother.

Her father, John Nightglow +, is in the town "peacekeep/police" force, the Spellbreakers, middle rank. This led him to be quite strict with Lucy's education (which she hated).

Her grandmother, Grace Nightglow, left Essuria when Lucy was just 5 years old, yet she remembers her fondly. She has druidic powers like Lucy does, and went out to explore and rediscover the Old Faith. Lucy is secretly hoping to meet her on her travels, as she feels quite alone with no (known) druids in her immediate circle.

Lucy does not have a lot of friends, but is friendly with other teens in the city, some of which are pursuing the arcane arts at the Academy. She never felt at home at the Academy however...

Further Adventures

Lucy's first goal had been to help cleanse nature for which she cared so deeply, as well as potentially finding others of her firbolg or druid kind, and perhaps finding her grandmother out in the wilds. Although this is still part of her, she has become disgusted by her guide Mammon Avari, disgusted with the engineer's guild's lies and secrecy, and the last thing she wants is to feel used. Her love for animals and nature has however not withered, and will fuel her actions to come, to fight anyone needlessly harming animals or nature. Yet now she wishes to attack those humans that have hurt, used and abused her, and will be wary of everyone except for her mother, Dovus Eilian +, Kemo Kantaki + and Crowe +.

Kemo has won her trust, and Crowe seems to be likeminded to Lucy in a way she cannot describe. She is however wary of Dovus, as she felt he had a hand in these circumstances.

**

Death

Lucy Nightglow died on April 17th, 664, at the age of 18, by the hand of the Subterranean Shambler, attempting to retrieve the Shard of Earth for Mammon Avari.
