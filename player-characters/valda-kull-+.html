Backstory

Valda Kull, born in the spring of 636, grew up in the village of Skar, up in the mountains to the West of Sheilwind. She loved roaming the woods with her little sister, and she loved fighting since she was a child.
When she was 22 and her younger sister 16, a band of brigands named the Skulkers roamed the outskirts of town. Her sister, Sonja Kull, was out berry picking when they found her. They beat her to death. Valda has since had an addiction to alcohol, and has since been out as a bounty hunter, hunting the outskirts of cities and towns as well as within cities taking on odd jobs. She's been muscle for hire for years and is 28 years old in 664. She's run with a group of mercenaries/musle for hire named The Fall. This group of mercenaries exists of 4 other members, all fighter/barbarian types like herself. Through her time in the wilds, she has learnt to survive out in the Lack.
Now she is on their trail, and knowing they're out there leads her to go beyond her limits to find them and avenge her sister.

In any case, GOLD is another big motivator for Valda, so whatever way she goes, she would probably want to have to have some coin at the end of it to make it worth her time.

Other than that, she's very brusque, loves playing at cards and dice and generally drinking the night away. Will make fun of "weaklings", basically anyone without big muscles or noticeable skill at killing.

Connections:

She's well known in Sheilwind and the surroundings as a very effective muscle for hire. People have learnt not to fuck with her, as she can have a short temper, but people generally respect her. Some of the richer families have hired her to protect their kin as they traverse around the city. She's been traversing the Lack for years now, and as a Wanderer she knows the terrain well as well as the potential dangers it holds.

She also has a good working relationship with the local guard, and especially Valda and Patrick Marvers have worked together numerous times. She is an authority figure among the rest of the guard/bounty hunter. She is however not very lawful, and is therefore a bit of a loose cannon compared to your average guard.

She spends most of her free time in taverns drinking, gambling, playing her lute and brawling with anyone who dares go against her. She's known to be intimidating, but never cruel. Therefore, she knows most tavern dwellers, and she often speaks with travellers who come through town and stay at the inn.

In her early twenties the used to run with a group of mercenaries named the Fall south(?) of the mountain range. In the beginning, her rage was hard to contain, but she managed to control her anger and direct it in a productive, destructive manner. However, during her time, she has rubbed some people in that area the wrong way and there may be some shadows from the past that would have reason to harm her.

Her relationship with her parents is OK. Since her sister's death, they've drifted apart from one another, and Valda hasn't seen them in years since she left, but she hopes they're doing OK.

Valda Kull was killed by a group of Skulkers on April 18th, 664, at the age of 28, after the party abandoned her.
