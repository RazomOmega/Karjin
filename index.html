Arts

#Location

An ancient library in Aqyla where forgotten arts can be learned. {REDACTED}

You may purchase books and scrolls to learn forgotten arts after a long rest. Each art can be purchased only once.

Fighter (Battlemaster)

70GP
Whirlwind

#Upgrade #Ability #Fighter #Battlemaster

Spend a superiority die while attacking to create a gust of wind originating from you. All creatures within 5ft. must make a Strength saving throw or be pushed away from you by 15ft., taking damage from the Superiority die.

100gp
Storm Surge
Storm Surge

#Upgrade #Ability #Fighter

Attacks made during your Action Surge deal an additional 1d12 Lightning damage.

120gp
Sunlit Confluence
Sunlit Confluence

#Upgrade #Ability #Fighter

Once per long rest, as a bonus action, choose a hostile creature and mark it with a ray of sunlight. Each of your allies may immediately spend their reaction to make an attack of opportunity against the target.

Rogue

50gp
Gust-step
Gust-step

#Upgrade #Ability #Rogue

Once per short rest, you may Dash as a free action. Your movement does not provoke attacks of opportunity this turn.

60gp
Vault of the Swan
Vault of the Swan

#Upgrade #Ability #Rogue

Once per short rest, you may glide 5ft. into the air for every 5ft. of horizontal movement you make. You can't take fall damage as a result of this jump. If you land on a creature this way, you land a guaranteed hit (no roll, no crits).

50gp
Snatch
Snatch

#Upgrade #Ability #Rogue

Once per short rest, on a successful Deception roll toward each onlooker, you may choose an uncarried object of 10 pounds or less that you can see within 30ft. and have it fly toward your hand unnoticed.

Warlock

30gp
Rite of Cloudless Sight
Rite of Cloudless Sight

#Upgrade #Ability #Warlock

You offer some life force to increase the acuity of your senses.
You may pay 5 HP at any time to gain the following bonuses for 1 minute:

  • You gain advantage on Perception and Insight checks.
  • Creatures have disadvantage on Stealth and Deception checks against you.
80gp
Dawn of Remembrance
Dawn of Remembrance

#Upgrade #Ability #Warlock

You offer some life force to accurately recall knowledge.
You may pay 10 HP once per long rest to either:

  • Un-prepare a spell and prepare another in its stead, or
  • Recall a piece of information you have heard with exact accuracy, by asking the DM.

Wizard

70gp
Fata Morgana
Fata Morgana

#Upgrade #Ability #Wizard

You learn the "Mirror Image" spell.
Whenever one of your Mirror Images is killed by a melee attack, you may use your reaction to cast a cantrip targeting the killer.

40gp
Voidglass Insight
Voidglass Insight

#Upgrade #Ability #Wizard

You may infuse any small gemstone using a 1st-level spellslot.

Construct

30gp
Sunforged Chassis
Sunforged Chassis

#Upgrade #Construct

Roll your to-hit die and gain max HP equal to the result + Constitution modifier.

80gp
Red Jasper Module
Red Jasper Module

#Upgrade #Item #Construct

Any time you deal damage with a melee attack, roll 1d4 and add that many charges to this module.
As a bonus action, touch a creature. It regains HP equal to the amount of charges.
Charges are depleted on use, or when finishing a short rest.

100gp
Diamond Module
Diamond Module

#Upgrade #Item #Construct

Once per short rest, you may spend a bonus action to create a 10ft. radius around you in which the air is distorted to make you look transparent. You and any creatures within the radius gain advantage on Stealth checks for 1 minute.

Anyone

Wisdom of the Dunes (400 gp)

You level up.
